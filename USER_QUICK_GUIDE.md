# 🎤 Microphone Sensitivity - Quick User Guide

## Problem: Audio Not Working?

If your microphone is **too sensitive** (picks up background noise) or **not sensitive enough** (doesn't detect your voice), follow these steps:

---

## ✅ Step 1: Test Your Microphone

1. Click the **"Test Mic"** button on the interview setup page
2. Allow microphone permissions when prompted
3. Wait 2-3 seconds while the system calibrates automatically
4. You should see:
   - ✅ Green checkmark
   - 🎵 Audio level bar
   - 🎚️ Sensitivity slider

---

## ✅ Step 2: Speak to Test

1. **Say something** out loud (e.g., "Hello, testing microphone")
2. Watch the audio level bar:
   - **Blue/Gray bar** = Your audio level
   - **Red line** = Detection threshold
   - **"✓ Voice Detected"** = Working correctly!

---

## ✅ Step 3: Adjust if Needed

### If Voice NOT Detected (bar doesn't reach red line):
→ **Slide RIGHT** (increase sensitivity)
- Or speak louder
- Or move closer to microphone

### If Too Much Background Noise Detected:
→ **Slide LEFT** (decrease sensitivity)
- Or reduce background noise
- Or use headphones with mic

## 🎯 Common Scenarios

### Scenario A: Quiet Microphone (Laptop/Budget Headset)
**Symptom**: Voice not detected, even when speaking loudly
**Solution**: 
- Slide sensitivity to **70-90%** (more sensitive)
- Speak directly into microphone
- Check system volume settings

### Scenario B: Too Sensitive (High-End Mic/Quiet Room)
**Symptom**: Background noise triggers detection constantly
**Solution**:
- Slide sensitivity to **20-40%** (less sensitive)
- Turn off fans, AC, music
- Close windows (traffic noise)

### Scenario C: Noisy Environment (Coffee Shop/Office)
**Symptom**: Background conversations trigger detection
**Solution**:
- Slide sensitivity to **10-30%** (much less sensitive)
- Use headphones with built-in mic
- Move to quieter location
- Speak louder than ambient noise

### Scenario D: Perfect Setup
**Symptom**: Green "Voice Detected" when speaking, nothing when silent
**Solution**: 
- ✅ No adjustment needed!
- Settings will be remembered for next time

---

## 💡 Tips

1. **Auto-Saves**: Your settings are automatically saved for next time
2. **Re-Test**: Can click "Stop" and "Test Mic" again to recalibrate
3. **During Interview**: If issues occur, exit and adjust sensitivity, then restart
4. **Best Practice**: Test in the same environment where you'll do the interview

---

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

| Problem | Quick Fix |
|---------|----------|
| No mic permission | Check browser address bar, click 🔒 icon, enable microphone |
| Calibration stuck | Reload page, try again |
| Settings not saving | Check you're not in incognito/private mode |
| Still not working | Try different browser (Edge recommended) |

---

## 📱 Device-Specific Tips

### MacBook Pro / iMac
- Usually very sensitive (50-70% works best)
- Built-in mic is high quality

### Windows Laptop
- Varies by model (40-60% typical)
- External USB mic recommended

### Gaming Headset
- Usually sensitive (60-80% works well)
- Good noise cancellation

### Bluetooth Earbuds
- Can be inconsistent (30-50%)
- Check battery level

### USB Webcam
- Quality varies (20-60%)
- Position close to you

---

## ✅ Success Indicators

You're ready to start when:
- ✅ Audio bar moves when you speak
- ✅ "Voice Detected" shows when speaking
- ✅ Nothing triggers when you're silent
- ✅ Background noise doesn't trigger it

---

## 🆘 Still Having Issues?

1. **Try a different browser** (Microsoft Edge recommended)
2. **Check system microphone settings** (not muted)
3. **Restart your device**
4. **Use external microphone/headset**
5. **Contact support** with details about your device

---

## 🎯 Remember

- **Louder is better** than too quiet
- **Quiet room** = better experience
- **Headset with mic** = most reliable
- **Test before interview** = avoid surprises
